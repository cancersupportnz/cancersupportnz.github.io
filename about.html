About us

About Us

NZ Cancer Support is a volunteer-led, non-profit organisation providing care and compassion for New Zealanders affected by cancer. Whether it’s emotional support, transport to treatment, or a comforting presence during hard times, we’re here for you.

Our mission is to build a community of support, resilience, and hope. We work hand in hand with local partners and healthcare providers to make sure no Kiwi faces cancer alone.
